<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Traits, Rerolls, and Unit Growth">

Once you have secured a few strong units, you can significantly boost their power through the Trait and Stat upgrade systems. However, as a beginner, you must manage these resources carefully.

### Upgrade Systems Explained
- **Trait Rerolls**: Changes a unit's passive trait, granting massive percentage boosts to damage, range, or attack speed.
- **Stat Rerolls & Locks**: Allows you to roll for better base stats (Damage, Range, Cooldown) and lock in desirable values while rerolling the rest.
- **Etherealize**: A mid-to-late game progression system that elevates a unit's base power ceiling using specialized shards.
- **Evolution**: Consumes specific materials and duplicate units to transform a base unit into its ultimate, highly powerful form.

| Progression Step | Resource Cost | Priority for Beginners |
|---|---|---|
| **Leveling** | Gold / Experience | **High** (Do this first) |
| **Trait Rerolling** | Trait Reroll Scrolls | **Medium** (Save for S-Tier units) |
| **Stat Locking** | Stat Locks | **Low** (Endgame optimization only) |
| **Evolution** | Crafting Materials | **High** (Target your main carry first) |

<Callout type="success" title="Smart Rerolling">
Only use your Trait Rerolls on units that you know will stay in your team for a long time, such as S-tier meta carries. Wasting rerolls on temporary rare or epic units will slow down your late-game progression.
</Callout>

</Section>
