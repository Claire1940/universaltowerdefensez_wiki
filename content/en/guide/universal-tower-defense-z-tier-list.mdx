<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution and Etherealize Guide">

To reach the full potential of any unit on the **universal tower defense z tier list**, you must engage with the growth systems: Leveling, Etherealizing, and Evolving. These layers of progression turn a standard summon into a meta-defining powerhouse.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Leveling">
    Use your units in Story Mode or Virtual Realm to reach their base level cap. This unlocks the ability to perform more advanced upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Etherealization">
    Use **Etherealization Shards** on units that are permanent fixtures of your team. This provides a significant stat boost and is a prerequisite for some evolutions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Material Farming">
    Collect specific evolution materials from Raids and Challenges. Each unit has unique requirements, often tied to their anime origin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">
    Evolve the unit to its final form. This usually changes its attack pattern, increases its range, and adds powerful new passives or active abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Tip">
Prioritize evolving your **Economy** units first if you find yourself struggling to afford upgrades mid-match. An evolved **Bulmo** or **Fastcart** can generate significantly more gold, allowing you to place your expensive S-tier carries much earlier.
</Callout>
