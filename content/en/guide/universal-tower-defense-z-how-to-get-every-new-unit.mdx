<Section icon={Shield} title="The Evolution and Etherealize Path">

Many of the "new" units in **Universal Tower Defense Z** cannot be summoned in their final form. Instead, you must summon a base version and progress through the **Evolution** and **Etherealize** systems. This is where most players get stuck, as it requires specific fragments and materials found only in late-game modes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon the Base Unit">
    Acquire the base version of a unit (e.g., Jinoo) from the summoning area using Gems or Tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Leveling to Max">
    Use the unit in **Story Mode** or **Infinite** to reach the level cap. You cannot evolve a unit that hasn't reached its base potential.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Evolution Materials">
    Farm **Raids** for specific fragments (Ocean, Sky, or Universal Fragments). Some units require "Alpha Swords" or "Ruler Souls."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolve and Etherealize">
    Access the Evolution menu to transform the unit. Use **Etherealization Shards** to further boost stats and unlock hidden passives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Material | Source Location | Associated Units |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Universal Fragments** | World Raids | Most S+ Tier Units |
| **Ocean Fragments** | Challenge Modes | Water-themed / Summer Units |
| **Sky Fragments** | Infinite Mode (Wave 50+) | Hill and Hybrid Units |
| **Ethereal Shards** | Ultra Boss Fights | All Etherealized Forms |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not waste your **Etherealization Shards** on B-Tier units. Evolution is expensive, and materials are scarce. Only invest in units labeled as **S+** or **S** in the tier list to ensure your team can handle the 2026 late-game content.
</Callout>
