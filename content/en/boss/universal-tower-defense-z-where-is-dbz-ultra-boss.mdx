The DBZ Ultra Boss features multiple phases, shield mechanics, and high-damage area-of-effect attacks that can disable your towers temporarily. Understanding these mechanics is the difference between a successful clear and a sudden defeat.

- **Shield Phase**: At 50% health, the boss gains a temporary shield. Focus on high-frequency attacks to break the shield quickly.
- **Rage Mode**: Below 30% health, the boss moves 25% faster. Save your stun abilities specifically for this phase to prevent leaks.
- **Stun Attacks**: The boss will periodically disable nearby units. Spread your carries out to avoid having your entire defense disabled at once.
