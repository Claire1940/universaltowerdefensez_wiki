  <Step num="3" title="The Kill Zone Formation">
    Identify a "bend" or a long straightaway on the map. This is your Kill Zone. Place your highest DPS units here, such as **The Strongest in History**. Surround them with support units like **Virtual Idol** or **Love Goddess** to boost their range and attack speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Active Management and Upgrading">

  </Step>
