To unlock the true potential of your roster in **universal tower defense z zoro**, you must utilize the game's deep progression systems. Leveling up your units via standard gameplay is only the first step; Etherealizing and Evolving are required to tackle high-wave Infinite runs and Ultra Bosses.

The step-by-step process below details how to efficiently transition a basic summon into an endgame powerhouse.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Core Carry">
    Identify a high-rarity unit (such as Triple Threat or King Sailor) that you plan to keep in your active roster long-term. Do not waste rare materials on temporary starter units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Max Out Base Levels">
    Farm Story Mode or replayable Virtual Realm stages to maximize the unit's base level. This ensures you understand their attack patterns and range before investing premium resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Etherealize for Power Spikes">
    Use Etherealization Shards on your chosen unit. Etherealizing provides a massive stat multiplier, allowing your unit to handle tougher enemy waves and bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gather Evolution Materials">
    Collect specific evolution items, fragments, and catalysts from Raids and Event Modes. Each major unit requires a unique recipe of materials to undergo Evolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Execute the Evolution">
    Evolve your unit to unlock a completely new form, featuring enhanced skills, wider attack areas (AOE), and superior base scaling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Success">
