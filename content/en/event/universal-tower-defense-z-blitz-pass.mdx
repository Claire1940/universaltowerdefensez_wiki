<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Universal Tower Defense Z Blitz Pass">

The battle pass system in **Universal Tower Defense Z** (formerly known as UTDX) serves as a primary progression engine for both free-to-play and premium players. By playing various game modes, completing tasks, and participating in seasonal events, you earn Experience Points (XP) that unlock progressive reward tiers. 

This system is designed to run alongside major updates, such as the massive **Update 4.0**, which introduced fresh summer-themed content, new units, and rich resource pools. Whether you are aiming for high-tier summon tickets, massive Gem stacks, or rare upgrade items, keeping up with your pass progression is essential to staying competitive in the late-game meta.

| Pass Track | Key Focus | Primary Rewards | Access Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free Track** | Base progression | Gems, gold, basic tickets, minor rerolls | Available to all players |
| **Premium Track** | Accelerated growth | Large reroll stacks, exclusive skins, Ruler tickets | Requires Robux purchase |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Upgrades with Pass Rewards">

Once you start collecting Trait Rerolls, Stat Locks, and Gems from the pass, you must apply them systematically. The endgame of **Universal Tower Defense Z** relies heavily on unit optimization.

### Unit Growth Priority Flow

1. **Leveling**: Use basic gameplay XP to max out your unit's base level.
2. **Etherealize**: Apply shards obtained from high-tier pass rewards to boost base statistics.
3. **Trait Rerolling**: Use your accumulated Trait Reroll stacks to hunt for top-tier traits like *Unrivaled*, *Omega*, or *Sage*.
4. **Stat Locking & Rerolling**: Lock in high damage or cooldown reduction percentages, then reroll the remaining minor stats.
5. **Evolution**: Gather the necessary evolution materials from Raids to transform your unit into its final, most powerful form.

| Unit Name | Role | Best Trait Fit | Upgrade Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Joyful Captain** | DPS / Support | Unrivaled / Sage | High |
