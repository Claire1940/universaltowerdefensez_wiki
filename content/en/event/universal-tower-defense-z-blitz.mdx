<Section icon={Zap} title="Unit Optimization: Traits, Stats, and Evolution">

A true **universal tower defense z blitz** requires more than just high-tier units; you need optimized stats. The difference between a base unit and one with a "Godly" trait can be over 300% in total damage output.

**Key Optimization Systems:**
- **Trait Rerolls**: Changes the unique passive of a unit (e.g., Godly, Reaper, Nimble).
- **Stat Rerolls**: Modifies the base Attack, Range, and Cooldown percentages.
- **Stat Locks**: Prevents a specific high-tier stat (like a 15% Damage roll) from being changed during a reroll.
- **Etherealize**: A direct power boost that consumes shards to raise a unit's ceiling.

| Upgrade Type | Resource Cost | Impact Level | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Traits** | Trait Rerolls | **Extreme** | Save for Mythics |
| **Stats** | Stat Rerolls | **High** | Roll for 10%+ ATK |
| **Etherealize** | Shards | **Medium** | Use on main carry |
| **Evolution** | Rare Mats | **Extreme** | Final power spike |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Reroll Strategy">
Never use Stat Locks on a unit unless you have already secured a top-tier Trait. A "Godly" trait with average stats is almost always better than a "Common" trait with perfect stats.
</Callout>
