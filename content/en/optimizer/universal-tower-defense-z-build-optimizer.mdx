<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait and Stat Optimization Strategy">

The true power of any unit comes from its Trait and Stat line. Using the **universal tower defense z build optimizer** mindset means you shouldn't settle for base stats. Traits are permanent modifiers that can drastically change a unit's performance. For instance, a "Godly" trait on an A-tier unit can sometimes outperform an S-tier unit with a "Common" trait.

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Warning">
Never spend your Trait Rerolls on starter units. Save them for Mythic or Evolved units that you plan to keep in your permanent rotation for Raids and World Bosses.
</Callout>

To optimize a specific unit, follow this systematic approach:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Assessment">
    Identify if the unit is a "Keeper." Check the latest Tier List to ensure the unit has longevity in the 2026 meta.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait Rerolling">
    Use Trait Rerolls until you hit a high-tier modifier like "Celestial" or "Unique." These provide the highest percentage boosts to damage and speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stat Tuning">
    Once the Trait is set, use Stat Rerolls to improve the base Attack, Range, and Cooldown. Aim for at least an "A" grade in the unit's primary stat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locking and Refining">
    Use Stat Locks to protect a perfect "S" grade stat while you continue to reroll the other two categories for a flawless build.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution and Etherealize: Post-Build Growth">

Once you have established your base team, the next step in the **universal tower defense z build optimizer** process is Evolution. Evolving a unit doesn't just increase its numbers; it often unlocks new abilities, larger AOE circles, or unique debuffs like Bleed or Stun.

Etherealizing is another layer of power. It requires Etherealization Shards, which are primarily farmed in Raids and high-level Challenges. This system allows you to break the level cap and further specialize your units for endgame content.

<Callout type="success" title="Evolution Tip">
Prioritize evolving your Economy units (like Bulmo) or your Main Carry. Better economy leads to faster upgrades in-game, which is vital for surviving Wave 50+ in Infinite mode.
</Callout>

| Upgrade Type | Resource Required | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Evolution** | Unit-specific Materials | New Movesets / Massive Stat Jump |
| **Etherealize** | Etherealization Shards | Level Cap Increase / Power Scaling |
| **Leveling** | XP Food / Gameplay | Linear Stat Growth |
| **Relics** | Relic Fragments | Specialized Equipment Buffs |

The synergy between Evolution and Traits is where the "Optimizer" really shines. An evolved unit with a Damage-focused trait becomes a "World Boss" killer, whereas an evolved support unit with a Speed-focused trait can keep enemies perma-stunned.
