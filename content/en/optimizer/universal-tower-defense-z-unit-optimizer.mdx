<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Unit Optimization Plan">

Optimizing a unit requires a systematic approach. Follow this step-by-step progression pipeline to maximize your team's combat power without wasting valuable upgrade materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Codes First">
    Before spending any resources, redeem all active game codes to secure free Trait Rerolls, Gems, Universal Gems, and Stat Locks. This provides a baseline pool of resources to plan your upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Level Up the Unit to Max">
    Take your chosen carry unit through Story Mode or farming stages to maximize its base level. Leveling increases core stats and ensures you do not waste premium rerolls on an underleveled unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reroll for Top-Tier Traits">
    Spend your Trait Rerolls to secure powerful offensive or utility traits. Prioritize traits that boost raw damage, reduce cooldowns, or increase attack range depending on the unit's archetype.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Stats with Stat Locks">
    Use Stat Rerolls to fine-tune the unit's stat lines. Once you land an S-rank stat (such as Critical Damage), use a Stat Lock to protect it while rerolling the remaining weaker stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Etherealize and Evolve">
    Gather the necessary evolution materials and Etherealization Shards from Raids and Challenges. Evolving your optimized unit unlocks its final, most devastating form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Success">
An evolved unit with locked S-rank stats can deal up to 300% more damage than its base form. Always complete the entire pipeline for your main carry before starting on your supports.
</Callout>

</Section>

Different game modes require distinct team compositions. An optimized team for Story Mode will often struggle in Infinite or World Raids due to scaling HP pools and different enemy mechanics.

### Mode-Specific Compositions

| Game Mode | Recommended Setup | Core Strategy | Priority Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Story Mode** | 1 Cheap Carry, 1 Farm, 1 Support | Rush early upgrades on your carry to clear waves quickly. | High (Start Here) |
| **Infinite Mode** | 2 Main Carries, 2 Supports, Bulmo | Stack slow and stun debuffs while scaling up your damage carries. | Medium (For Gems) |
| **Raids / Bosses** | 1 Boss Killer, 1 Buffer, 1 Debuffer | Bring high single-target DPS and defense-shredding support units. | High (For Materials) |
| **Virtual Realm** | 1 Hybrid Carry, Fastcart, 1 Support | Focus on consistent, repeatable wave clearing with minimal setup. | Medium (For Grinding) |
