<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Trait and Stat Optimization">

The **universal tower defense z meta optimizer** isn't just about which units you have, but how you've tuned their internal stats. Traits and Stat Rerolls are the primary ways to push a unit from "good" to "broken." Players often make the mistake of spreading their reroll resources too thin across their entire roster.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Never use **Trait Rerolls** on units below A-tier unless you are stuck in early Story mode. These resources are scarce and should be reserved for Mythic and Evolved units that will stay in your team for months.
</Callout>

To effectively optimize a unit, follow this priority list for stats and traits:

| Resource | Primary Goal | Recommended Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trait Reroll** | Unlock unique passive buffs | Main DPS / Boss Killers |
| **Stat Reroll** | Maximize Damage and Crit | Carries and Hybrid Units |
| **Stat Lock** | Protect 90%+ rolls | Only for S+ Tier units |
| **Relic Reroll** | Optimize equipment synergy | Late-game Raid builds |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Selection">
    Identify a unit that has long-term viability (e.g., an S-tier carry). Do not invest heavily in starter units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait Alignment">
    Use Trait Rerolls until you land a high-tier damage or utility trait. Traits that increase Range or Cooldown Reduction are vital for supports.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stat Polishing">
    Once the trait is set, use Stat Rerolls. If you hit a near-max Damage or Critical Damage stat, use a **Stat Lock** to protect it while you roll for the remaining slots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
For scaling units like **Jinoo (Shadow Monarch)**, focus heavily on Cooldown Reduction. The faster they attack, the quicker they reach their maximum scaling potential in Infinite modes.
</Callout>
