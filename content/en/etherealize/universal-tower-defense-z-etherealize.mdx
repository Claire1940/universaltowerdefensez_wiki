<Section icon={Target} title="The Etherealize and Evolution Process">

Transitioning your roster from basic summons to evolved legends requires a disciplined approach to resource spending. The game provides multiple layers of growth: leveling, trait rolling, etherealizing, and finally, evolving. Following a specific order ensures you don't waste precious **Stat Locks** or **Trait Rerolls** on units that you might replace later.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unit Selection and Leveling">
    Before investing in the **universal tower defense z etherealize** process, ensure the unit has long-term meta value. Use normal progression to raise the unit's base level. This allows you to test their performance in Story Mode before committing rare materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait and Stat Optimization">
    Once a unit is leveled, use your **Trait Rerolls** earned from codes. Aim for high-tier traits that complement the unit's role (e.g., Damage for DPS, Cooldown for Support). Use **Stat Locks** to protect perfect rolls while chasing a better overall stat line.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Etherealization Transformation">
    Collect **Etherealization Shards** from Raids and Challenges. Apply these to your chosen unit to trigger the Etherealize state. This will significantly raise their power ceiling and prepare them for the final evolution step.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Gathering for Evolution">
    Each unit requires specific evolution materials, such as **Universal Fragments**, **Ocean Fragments**, or **Sky Fragments**. These are typically found in themed Raid stages or purchased using **Universal Gems**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Evolution and Testing">
    Execute the Evolution to unlock the unit's ultimate form. Immediately test the upgraded unit in the **Virtual Realm** or high-wave **Infinite Mode** to confirm the improvements in clear speed and boss damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Warning">
Evolution is often permanent and consumes the base materials. Ensure your unit has the desired traits *before* evolving if the evolution changes the base stat requirements. Always check the official Discord for the latest balance changes before a major upgrade.
</Callout>
